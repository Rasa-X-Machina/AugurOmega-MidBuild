# Augur Omega: Example Scenarios for Recalibrated Growth System

## Scenario 1: Tech Startup Scaling User Acquisition

### Background
- **Company**: SaaS startup with 50 employees, $2M ARR, growing 20% MoM
- **Challenge**: Need to scale customer acquisition while maintaining unit economics
- **Growth Objective**: Reach $10M ARR within 18 months

### Agent Team Formation
```
Marketing Intelligence Agent: Specialized in customer acquisition
Growth Optimization Agent: Focuses on scaling strategies
ROI Verification Agent: Tracks acquisition cost and LTV
UX Personalization Agent: Optimizes conversion flows
Revenue Forecasting Agent: Projects growth trajectories
```

### User Journey Execution

#### Stage 1: Onboarding & Profiling
**User Action**: CEO completes enhanced onboarding
- Selects "Growth Stage" company type
- Specifies "User Acquisition" primary growth objective
- Chooses "Risk-Averse Growth" payment preference (deferred payment qualified)

**System Response**:
- ROI calculator shows projected acquisition cost improvements: 35% reduction
- Qualifies for 4-month payment deferral
- Forms specialized agent team based on profile

#### Stage 2: Value Discovery
**Marketing Intelligence Agent** analyzes current acquisition stack:
- Identifies 3 underperforming channels with high potential
- Projects $50K monthly savings in acquisition cost
- Recommends reallocation of 40% of budget to high-performing channels

**Growth Optimization Agent** suggests:
- New customer segmentation based on LTV prediction
- Personalized onboarding flows for each segment
- Predictive churn prevention for high-value segments

#### Stage 3: Implementation & Value Tracking
**UX Personalization Agent** implements:
- Dynamic pricing experiments for different segments
- Personalized trial experiences based on use case
- Behavioral email sequences for engagement

**ROI Verification Agent** tracks:
- Baseline: $50 CAC, 7x LTV/CAC
- Week 4: $35 CAC, 9x LTV/CAC (30% improvement)
- Week 8: $32 CAC, 10x LTV/CAC (36% improvement)

#### Stage 4: Payment & Scaling
- **Value Realized**: $18K monthly savings in acquisition costs
- **Payment Trigger**: After 3 months of verified improvement
- **Billing**: $2K base + 5% of value generated (900/month)
- **Expansion**: Adds retention optimization features

### Financial Impact
- **Month 1-4**: $0 payments due to deferral
- **Month 5-12**: $900/month (5% of $18K savings)
- **Annual Value**: $18K x 12 = $216K in acquisition cost savings
- **Platform Cost**: $2K x 12 + ($900 x 8) = $31.2K
- **ROI**: 6:1 with payment deferral option
