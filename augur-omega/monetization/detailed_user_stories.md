# Augur Omega: Detailed User Stories for Growth-Oriented Platform

## Role-Based User Stories

### 1. Growth-Focused CEO Stories

#### Job Story: When facing competitive pressure, I want to leverage AI to accelerate growth, so that I can maintain market position and increase market share.

**US-CEO-001: Value-Driven Platform Selection**
```
As a Growth-Focused CEO,
I want an AI platform with proven ROI metrics and deferred payment options,
So that I can implement AI capabilities without upfront financial risk while accelerating growth.
```
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Platform provides ROI calculator with industry benchmarks
  - Deferred payment option available up to 6 months for qualified companies
  - Clear value attribution for AI-driven decisions
  - Success stories from similar companies in my industry
- **Priority:** Critical
- **Estimate:** 8 story points
- **Dependencies:** Value tracking system, customer success team

**US-CEO-002: Growth Strategy Integration**
```
As a Growth-Focused CEO,
I want AI recommendations that align with my growth strategy,
So that I can focus resources on the highest-impact growth opportunities.
```
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - AI system understands company growth stage and objectives
  - Provides prioritized list of growth opportunities
  - Tracks progress toward growth goals
  - Adjusts recommendations based on performance
- **Priority:** High
- **Estimate:** 13 story points
- **Dependencies:** Growth analytics engine, user profile system

**US-CEO-003: Stakeholder Communication**
```
As a Growth-Focused CEO,
I want clear reporting on AI-driven growth metrics,
So that I can communicate value to board members and investors.
```
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Executive dashboard with key growth metrics
  - Board-ready reports with clear attribution
  - Investment payback period calculations
  - Risk-adjusted growth projections
- **Priority:** High
- **Estimate:** 5 story points
- **Dependencies:** Analytics engine, reporting system
